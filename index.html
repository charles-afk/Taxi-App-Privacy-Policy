Privacy Policy

Please take the time to read through this very brief privacy policy concerning
the Taxi App Demo used to test out Google API's concerning Google Maps.
This Policy describes how data is collected, and used within the app. The Taxi
App Demo takes user input from a list of locations and then proceeds to show
the given location on a map, calculating the distance between two given locations,
and offers prices for the given trip simulating a taxi service. No data is collected
or stored besides the static information stored for favorite locations.
